What is this game?

Track the Word is a music-based lyric game that challenges you to find a song containing a specific word. Whether you're playing solo, with a friend, or competitively, the game makes song-based challenges fast and fun! ddd

This idea was inspired by Hayford's workplace game, where coworkers tried to name songs based on random words. However, checking lyrics manually slowed things down. Track the Word automates that process with smart tech, verifying songs instantly using lyrics databases.

Our goal is to keep the game dynamic, exciting, and competitive by removing delays and boosting the fun.
